How it works

We built Elefend to give you and your loved ones peace of mind, helping you prevent phone fraudsters from scamming you.

Monitoring

Analyzing and detecting

Intervening

Once a possible scam is detected, Elefend intervenes in real time, preventing you from becoming a scam victim

We built Elefend because everyone deserves to feel safe

Elefend is a security company with a mission to help the elderly and other vulnerable people to fight phone fraud and scams. Elefend uses advanced technology to monitor and analyze phone calls while they're happening (in real time), so that scams are prevented before it's too late.
